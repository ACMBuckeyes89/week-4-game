♢Crystals Collector♢

Directions

A random number will pop out onto the screen.
By clicking on the any of the four crystals below, you will be adding to your total point score.
If your total score equals to the random number, you win! Otherwise if your total score goes above the random number, you lose the game.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on them.
Everytime the game starts, the values of the crystals will change.

Good Luck!
